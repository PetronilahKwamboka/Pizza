About Us

Our Story

Petie's Pizza was started in March 2019 with the main purpose of serving people around our community with various delicacies of pizza.

Our location was inspired by the high number of college students around who were mainly our target market, but through marketing research and implementation, we have also acquired customers from various parts of Nairobi County.

Our pizzas are always praised by our customers, not to brag but seriously they are. Don't take our word for it though. Try one and see!!
